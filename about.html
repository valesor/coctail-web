who
we are

We’re a small, independently run bar in the fantastic area offering a comfortable atmosphere with superb drinks and service. We offer a range of unique cocktails you won’t find anywhere else.

Our bar also provides a wide, ever-changing range of craft beer, along with cider, wine and a large collection of spirits.

At our bar, we offer a wide variety of cocktails made with high-quality ingredients, from classics like the Mojito to exclusive creations. Our bartenders blend innovative techniques with the finest liquors to achieve the perfect balance in every drink.

We also have a diverse selection of beverages for all tastes, from smooth and fruity options to more intense and sophisticated drinks. Quality and presentation are our top priorities, ensuring a unique experience in every sip. Discover new combinations and enjoy to the fullest!
